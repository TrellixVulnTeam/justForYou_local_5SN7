Mission Statement

We, at Just For You Children's Centre, strive to provide an environment that nurtures and educates.
Our wish is to provide a stimulating place where children are free to explore their creativity and individuality. Our goal is to foster independence and social and emotional grouth through a play based learning environment.
We support families by encouraging open communication through an open door policy.
We provide an environment rich in literature, giving plenty of exposure to pre-writing and reading skills.
Our program provides children with hands on experiences with opportunities to explore while learning with
emphasis on early literacy, early numeracy, science, social studies and art.
Our materials provide children with the opportunity to learn through play. These will stimulate and challenge, help to develop perception and thinking skills as well as learn concepts and develop fin/gross motor movements.
